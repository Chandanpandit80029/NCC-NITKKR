Unit Overview

2 Haryana Battalion NCC

Our NCC unit at NIT Kurukshetra has been a cornerstone of leadership development and character building for students since its establishment. We take pride in molding young minds into responsible citizens and future leaders of our nation.

With a rich legacy of excellence, our unit has consistently produced cadets who have excelled in various fields, including defense services, civil services, and corporate leadership.

Associate NCC Officer

Dr. S. Kumar

Associate NCC Officer

With over 15 years of dedicated service in the NCC, Dr. S. Kumar has been instrumental in shaping the lives of hundreds of cadets. His commitment to excellence and passion for youth development has earned him several accolades and recognition at both state and national levels.

"The NCC is not just an organization; it's a movement that transforms young individuals into responsible citizens and leaders. My mission is to guide our cadets through this transformative journey, instilling in them the values of discipline, patriotism, and selfless service."

- Dr. S. Kumar, ANO
