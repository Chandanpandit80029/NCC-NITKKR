Eligibility Criteria

Age Requirements

  • Junior Division: 13-18 years
  • Senior Division: 18-26 years
  • Must be a student of the institution
  • No age relaxation for any category

Educational Qualifications

  • Junior Division: Class 8th to 10th
  • Senior Division: Class 11th onwards
  • Must maintain minimum 60% attendance
  • Good academic record preferred

Physical Fitness

  • Medically fit with no chronic illnesses
  • Minimum height: 152 cm for boys, 147 cm for girls
  • Minimum weight: 42 kg for boys, 40 kg for girls
  • Good eyesight (6/6 with or without glasses)

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there any fee for joining NCC?

No, NCC is completely free of cost. All training, uniforms, and equipment are provided by the government at no charge to cadets.

What is the time commitment required?

Cadets are expected to attend regular parades (approx. 4 hours per week) and participate in annual training camps (10-15 days per year). The commitment is designed to not interfere with academic schedules.

Can I join NCC if I wear glasses?

Yes, candidates with corrected vision (6/6 with glasses) are eligible to join NCC. However, certain specialized training may have additional vision requirements.

What are the career benefits of NCC?

NCC cadets receive preference in defense services, government jobs, and many private sector positions. The 'C' certificate provides bonus marks in several competitive examinations.
