Welcome from the Commanding Officer

The National Cadet Corps is one of the largest youth organizations in the world. It aims to develop character, comradeship, discipline, a secular outlook, and the ideals of selfless service among young citizens.

Our college NCC unit has been consistently producing disciplined and responsible cadets who have excelled in various fields. I invite all eligible students to join this prestigious organization and be part of this transformative journey.

- Col. R. Sharma, Commanding Officer

About NCC

History

The National Cadet Corps was established in 1948 under the National Cadet Corps Act. It has since grown to become one of the largest uniformed youth organizations in the world.

Vision

To build a motivated and organized youth leadership that provides the nation with dedicated volunteers in all walks of life and always available for the service of the nation.

Objectives

To develop character, comradeship, discipline, leadership, secular outlook, spirit of adventure, and ideals of selfless service amongst the youth of the country.

Eligibility & Benefits

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Indian citizen
  • Age between 13-26 years
  • Enrolled as a student in an educational institution
  • Medically fit

Benefits of Joining NCC:

  • NCC 'A', 'B', and 'C' Certificates
  • Grace marks in various competitive exams
  • Preference in defense services recruitment
  • Opportunities for youth exchange programs
  • Scholarships and financial assistance
